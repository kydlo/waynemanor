About

Hi, I'm Kyle Louis and I like to take pictures! I see a beautiful city and the brilliant people rising from this abyss. I see the lives, for which I lay down my life. That is the far, far better thing that I do than I have ever done. That is a far, far better rest that I go to than I have ever known. Dr. Crane isn't here right now. But if you'd like to make an appointment. I know exactly who he was. He was the Batman. Bats frighten me. It's time my enemies shared my dread.
